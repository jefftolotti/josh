Costumes & Props

Apparition mask, 2023
Made using burlap, liquid latex, and a homemade fabric acrylic, prior to being cut for the rest of the costume

Giant scythe, 2022
Scythe blade made with cut and sanded plywood that was spray painted and then dry brushed, attached to existing cut tree branch with screws and covered by rope

Apparition mask on the full costume, painted and cut to mimic the look of an apparition caught on camera while also blending into the foliage of the outdoor haunt

Zombie apocalypse clothing, 2022
Made for a cemetery caretaker character who fights off zombies, the worn clothing and blood effects were done with sandpaper, tea bags, acrylic paint, and coffee powder

Zombie clippers, 2023
Hedge clippers used to fight zombies. Blades were made of sanded plywood, spray painted and then dry brushed. Handles made from cut broom handles.
